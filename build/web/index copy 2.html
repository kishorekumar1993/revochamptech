Frequently Asked Questions

What is Flutter and why should I learn it?

Flutter is Google's open-source UI toolkit for building natively compiled apps across mobile, web, desktop, and embedded platforms — all from a single Dart codebase. It's fast, expressive, and backed by a massive ecosystem, making it one of the top cross-platform frameworks to learn in 2024.

Are the tutorials free?

Yes — every Flutter tutorial, Dart guide, and UI example on Revochamp Tech is completely free with no account required.

Which state management solutions are covered?

We cover GetX, Riverpod, Provider, BLoC, and plain setState — with practical, real-world examples for each so you can choose the right tool for every project.

Do I need programming experience to start?

No prior experience is needed. Our beginner track starts with Dart fundamentals, Flutter installation, and your first widget, then progressively builds toward production-level app architecture.
